Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TokoyoMusubi Shrine handles information when you use this mobile application.

1. Information I Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

TokoyoMusubi Shrine does not require you to create an account or provide personal contact information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

The app uses anonymous authentication (Firebase Authentication) to sync your progress. This generates a random anonymous user ID that is not linked to your identity.

The app stores the following types of data:

  • App preferences and settings (stored locally on your device)
  • In-app item status, omikuji history, and worship statistics (stored in the cloud via Firebase, linked to your anonymous user ID)

1.2 Information Related to Advertising

The app displays advertisements using third-party services. These services may collect certain information automatically in accordance with their own privacy policies, such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising identifiers
  • General usage data

TokoyoMusubi Shrine does not directly access, store, or control this information.

2. Face Data

What Face Data Is Collected

This app uses on-device face detection to detect:

  • Face position and head tilt (for bow detection)
  • Eye open/closed state (for prayer detection)

This data is processed entirely on your device in real-time.

How Face Data Is Used

Face data is used solely to detect gestures during worship (bowing and prayer). It is not used for identification or any other purpose.

Data Storage

Face data is processed in memory only and is not stored on your device or any server.

Data Retention

Face data is discarded immediately after each frame is processed. No face data is retained.

Third-Party Sharing

Face data is not shared with any third parties. No images or facial feature data are transmitted to external servers.

Usage Statistics

The app records cumulative usage statistics for your account, such as:

  • Number of successful bows
  • Total seconds with eyes closed during prayer

These statistics are stored in Firebase (cloud service) and linked to your anonymous user ID. They do not contain any facial images or biometric data.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Face Detection Technologies

This app uses the following technologies for on-device face detection. All processing occurs locally on your device.

Apple ARKit (iOS)

On iOS devices with TrueDepth camera (Face ID-enabled devices), the app uses Apple ARKit for high-precision face tracking. ARKit processes facial data entirely on your device.

  • ARKit Documentation
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Google ML Kit (iOS fallback / Android)

On iOS devices without TrueDepth camera and on all Android devices, the app uses Google ML Kit for face detection.

  • Google ML Kit Terms of Service
  • Google Privacy Policy

3.2 Advertising Services

Advertisements may be provided through third-party services such as Google AdMob.

These services may process information independently in order to deliver ads. For details, please refer to:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

3.3 Apple App Store

App distribution and any purchases are handled by Apple. TokoyoMusubi Shrine does not store payment information.

  • Apple Privacy Policy

3.4 Firebase Services

This app uses Firebase (provided by Google) for the following purposes:

  • Firebase Authentication: Anonymous sign-in to sync your progress across sessions
  • Cloud Firestore: Storing your app data (omikuji history, worship statistics) in the cloud
  • Firebase Remote Config: Delivering app configuration updates without requiring an app update
  • Firebase Analytics: Understanding app usage patterns to improve the experience (opt-in only)
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collecting crash reports to improve app stability (opt-in only)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Delivering push notifications about updates and announcements

Data is stored on Google's servers. For details, please refer to:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy

4. Your Privacy Choices

Camera Permission

You may control camera access through your device settings. If camera access is disabled, gesture-based features will be unavailable.

Advertising Preferences

You may manage ad personalization through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

5. Data Retention

Local app data is stored on your device and will be removed when you uninstall the app.

Cloud data (stored in Firebase) is retained as long as your anonymous account exists. If you wish to delete your cloud data, please contact us via email.

Face data is not retained; it is discarded immediately after processing each camera frame.

6. International Data Handling

Third-party services may process data in countries outside your own, including the United States, in accordance with their privacy policies.

7. Security

Reasonable technical measures are taken to protect data handled by the app. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last Updated" date will reflect any changes.
