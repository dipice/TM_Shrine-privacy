Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how TokoyoMusubi Shrine collects, uses, and shares information when you use this mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use TokoyoMusubi Shrine. The app stores only:

  • App preferences and settings stored locally on your device

1.2 Information Collected by Advertising Partners

When you use the app, our advertising partners may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Advertising Data: Information collected by advertising partners (see Section 3)

2. Camera Usage

Important: This app uses your device camera for gesture detection (such as bowing). All camera processing is performed entirely on your device using Google ML Kit. Camera images and facial detection data are never transmitted to external servers and are not stored.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google ML Kit

This app uses Google ML Kit for face detection to enable gesture-based interactions. All processing is performed on-device, and no facial data is sent to external servers.

  • ML Kit Terms of Service
  • Google Privacy Policy

3.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect and use information about you to provide personalized ads. This includes:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address and geographic location
  • Device information and app usage data

For more information about how Google uses your data, please see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

3.3 Apple App Store

App downloads and any future purchases are processed by Apple. We do not store your payment information. Please refer to:

  • Apple Privacy Policy

4. Your Privacy Choices

4.1 Camera Permissions

You can control camera access through your device settings. Denying camera permission will disable gesture-based features, but you can still use other app features.

4.2 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Object: Object to data processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

4.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

4.4 Advertising Choices

You can control personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track (disable)
  • In-App: When prompted by the consent dialog, you can choose to limit ad personalization

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the services and comply with legal obligations. Most data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

6. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Consent Management

When you first launch the app, you will be presented with a consent dialog (for users in applicable regions) where you can:
