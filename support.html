Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1. How do I use the shrine visit features?

1. Allow camera access when prompted (optional but recommended)

2. Navigate through the shrine by tapping the screen

3. At the main hall, you can make offerings and bow

4. Draw an omikuji (fortune) to receive your fortune

Q2. Why does the app need camera access?

The camera is used to detect your bowing gesture for an interactive experience. This processing happens entirely on your device - no images or facial data are sent to any server.

Camera access is optional. You can still use the app without it.

Q3. Is my camera data being sent somewhere?

No. All camera processing is performed locally on your device using Google ML Kit. Your camera images and facial detection data are never transmitted to external servers and are not stored.

Q4. What is omikuji?

Omikuji are fortune-telling paper strips found at Japanese shrines. In this app, you can draw a virtual omikuji to receive a fortune ranging from "Daikichi" (great blessing) to "Daikyo" (great curse).

Note: The fortunes are for entertainment purposes only.

Q5. Is this a real shrine?

No. "Tokoyomusubi Shrine" is a fictional virtual shrine created for this app. It is not affiliated with any real religious organization or shrine.

Q6. How do I remove ads?

Currently, there is no option to remove ads in this version of the app. We appreciate your understanding.

Q7. The bowing detection doesn't work properly. What should I do?

For best results:

  • Make sure you're in a well-lit environment
  • Position your face clearly in the camera view
  • Bow slowly and deliberately
  • Ensure no obstructions are blocking your face

If problems persist, try restarting the app.

Q8. How do I change the app language?

The app automatically uses your device's language setting. To change the language, change your device's system language in Settings.

Q9. Is my personal data safe?

Yes. The app does not collect or store personal information. Camera data is processed locally and never leaves your device. All app settings are stored locally on your device. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

Q10. How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! Please email us at lexipecid@gmail.com with:

  • A description of the bug or feature request
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Screenshots (if applicable)
