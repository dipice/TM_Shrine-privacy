Support

If you have any questions or need assistance with TokoyoMusubi Shrine, please refer to the FAQ below or contact us by email.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the app?
  1. Allow camera access when prompted (optional)
  2. Navigate through the shrine by tapping the screen
  3. At the main hall, you can perform in-app worship actions and bow
Why does the app request camera access?

The camera is used to detect bowing gestures as part of the worship flow.

All processing is performed entirely on your device. No images or facial data are transmitted or stored.

Camera access is optional, and the app can be used without it.

Is my camera data sent anywhere?

No.

All camera processing occurs locally on your device. No images or facial detection data leave your device.

Can I remove ads?

There is currently no option to remove ads in this version.

Thank you for your understanding.

The gesture detection doesn't seem to work. What should I do?

For best results:

  • Ensure you are in a well-lit environment
  • Position your face clearly within the camera view
  • Bow slowly and deliberately
  • Avoid obstructions in front of your face

If issues persist, please try restarting the app.

Is my personal data safe?

TokoyoMusubi Shrine does not collect or store personal information.

Camera data is processed locally on your device and never leaves it. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Please contact us by email with the following information:

  • A description of the issue or suggestion
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Screenshots, if applicable
